Convey of Hope

As a faith-based nonprofit organization Convey of Hope has helped more than 10million people throughout india by sharing Food,Water,Emergency supplies,Education know-how and opportunities that empower people to live independent lives ,free from poverty ,disease and hunger

Convey of Hope does this through : Mobilizing tens of thousands of volunteers each year Partnering with churches ,business ,individuals and other hamnization organizations who are intent on doing good work among the improverished and suffering

Transparency -- we have received the prestigious Fours Star Charity Award from Charity Navigator for 14 years

Convoy of Hope also operates a Women's Empowerment program that is designed to educate women and girls in vulnerable communities. It also provides entrepreneurial training and seed capital for women to start their own businesses
